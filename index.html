Vkarma is an educational App which is designed very carefully and under the guidance of the professional teachers and trainers. Its main aim is the solve the problem of the student with the revolutionary idea of providing the student what they want and also simplifying the administration to enhance the output. It also provide the mechanism for parents to monitor the progress of their child.
It too deal with the problem of communication gap between teacher and parents and also provide a way to provide instant information all in one app.
